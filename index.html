Some History

In 1994 I started collecting information on programs which aim to reduce hunger and homlessness in Toronto. At one point I had 1100 records of meal programs, helper and health centres and social service agencies.

The Government of Ontario removed the rental housing registry and those attendant rent controls in the mid 1980's

Now they are going to eliminate any appearance of the fair play social contract and just turn control over to property speculator friends.
